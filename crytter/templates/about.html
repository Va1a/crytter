About

dKYP is a peer-to-peer trading platform.

It allows users to post offers and search through them in an effort to make peer-to-peer trading easier.

dKYP DOES NOT act as a middleman OR provide any guarantee for users, but instead allows them to find and create offers, and allows users to provide contact information to make the trade happen.

All trading which does happen takes place between the independent individuals themselves. dKYP only acts as a "search engine" of offers for users.

Take every precaution whilst dealing online, and ensure that you are not going to be scammed! Scamming is rampant in the peer-to-peer trading community.

Please read and agree to our Terms & Conditions as well as our Rules & Guidelines to use dKYP.net
