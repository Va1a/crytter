crytter.org Rules & Guidelines

Last updated: April 16, 2021

To provide a more fair and fun environment for our users and guests, we have established a list of rules each guest and user is required to follow, not following these rules can result in account suspension or removal.

By accessing crytter.org (henceforth referred to as the Site), you express approval of these rules as well as our Terms & Conditions. Violating ANY of these rules may cause your account to be permanently terminated.

All Posted Content

1) Is not to contain phrases which insult a wider demographic of people.

2) Is not to contain references to illegal activity.

3) Is not to contain any personal information of any person other than your own (INCLUDING other social media handles, contact information, etc.)

4) Is not to be posted with the intent of misleading other users.

5) Is not to be posted without meaning or intent to positively contribtue to the Site, and cannot advertise other websites.

6) MUST be posted by an actual human, and cannot be automated in any way.

Ratings

1) Users are not to post false reviews/ratings if they have not had a direct, firsthand deal experience with the user being rated.

2) Reviews should not contain any false or misleading statements or references to illegal activity.

Biographies

1) We provide biographies as a place for users to write information concerning themselves.

2) Biographies MAY contain YOUR OWN (and ONLY your own) contact information. Physical addresses and locations are not allowed. We recommend leaving out true first and last names in favor of an alias, and using social media accounts as points of contact.

Comments

1) Are to directly relate to the post they are being commented on in some way, including expressing desire to carry out the related offer.

2) Are not to falsely defame the poster (sales trashing).

3) Are not to falsely claim interest in the offer being provided by the post.

Offers

1) Are not related to crytter.org in any way, and are posted by other users to be viewed by other users.

2) Should express true intent and interest in carrying out the deal which they relate to. (Do not offer if you do not want to do the deal).

3) Should be removed if the poster is no longer interested in pursuing the offer.
