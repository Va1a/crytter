Exchange Rates?

The exchange rates sidebar displays updated exchange rates as provided by Coinbase.

JavaScript must be enabled in the browser for rates to display. Rates update every 30 seconds, without the need for a page refresh.

The exchange rate of a currency reflects the current price, in US dollars, of that currency.

Rates increase as demand / perceived value of a currency increases, and decrease if the opposite happens.

Similarly, offers display their current deal value.
